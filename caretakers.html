Our Services

  • homeSocial and household services
    • Social services provide assistance to those unable to care for themselves due to health conditions, including purchasing essential goods, cooking, eating, and we also provide bathhouse accommodations for those without hot water supply.
    • Social services provide assistance in vacation preparation, laundry, repair, and return services. We also provide fuel, heating, and water delivery for residential residents, and cleaning for those unable to care for themselves.
  • local_hospitalSocial and medical Services
    • consulting on social and medical issues;
    • systematic monitoring of the recipient of social services in order to identify deviations in their health;
    • implementation of procedures related to the organization of care for persons who are unable to independently care for themselves due to their health condition;
    • ensuring that the recipient of social services takes medications in accordance with the doctor's prescription;
    • implementation of activities aimed at the formation of a healthy lifestyle;
  • account_balanceSocial and legal services
    • assistance in preparing documents and restoring lost documents of the recipient of social services;
    • assistance in obtaining a compulsory health insurance policy assistance in preparing documents required for placement in a stationary social services organization;
    • assistance in restoring lost (preserving occupied) housing, inheritance;
    • assistance in obtaining legal services (including free of charge);
    • assistance in protecting the rights and legitimate interests of the recipient of social services.
